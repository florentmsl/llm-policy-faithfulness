Kangaroo (Atari 2600). A mother kangaroo climbs a three-level tree structure to reach her kidnapped joey at the top.

Hazards:
- Monkeys on the platforms throw coconuts horizontally at the player.
- Falling coconuts drop from above.
- Platforms are connected by ladders; some platforms have fruit to punch for bonus points.
- A bell at the top can be punched to drop fruit bonuses.

Controls (action codes):
- 0 NOOP
- 1 FIRE (punch)
- 2 UP
- 3 RIGHT
- 4 LEFT
- 5 DOWN
- 6 UPRIGHT (jump up-right)
- 7 UPLEFT (jump up-left)

Relevant observed objects (from the SCoBots feature set):
Player1 (mother kangaroo), Child1 (joey), Monkey1..Monkey3, FallingCoconut1, ThrownCoconut1, Ladder1..Ladder3, Platform1..Platform3, Fruit1..Fruit2, Bell1.

Coordinate convention: y increases downward on screen; lower y means higher on the tree, so reaching the joey requires y to decrease over time.

The episode ends when the player loses all lives (from coconut hits) or reaches the joey at the top.
