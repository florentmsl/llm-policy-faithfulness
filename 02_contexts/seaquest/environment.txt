Seaquest (Atari 2600). A submarine moves left/right and up/down underwater, shooting torpedoes at sharks and collecting divers.

Hazards and objects:
- Sharks (Shark1..SharkN) swim horizontally and damage the submarine on contact.
- Divers (Diver1..DiverN) must be picked up by colliding with them; a full cargo of divers earns bonus points when the submarine surfaces.
- PlayerMissile is the torpedo fired by the submarine; kills sharks on hit.
- The submarine has an oxygen meter that decreases over time; surfacing refills it. Running out of oxygen loses a life.

Controls (full Atari action space, 18 actions):
- 0 NOOP, 1 FIRE, 2 UP, 3 RIGHT, 4 LEFT, 5 DOWN,
- 6 UPRIGHT, 7 UPLEFT, 8 DOWNRIGHT, 9 DOWNLEFT,
- 10 UPFIRE, 11 RIGHTFIRE, 12 LEFTFIRE, 13 DOWNFIRE,
- 14 UPRIGHTFIRE, 15 UPLEFTFIRE, 16 DOWNRIGHTFIRE, 17 DOWNLEFTFIRE.

Relevant observed objects (from the SCoBots feature set):
Player1 (submarine), Shark1..Shark10, Diver1..Diver4, PlayerMissile1, CollectedDiver1..CollectedDiver4.

Coordinate convention: y increases downward. Surfacing corresponds to y approaching 0.

An episode ends when all lives are lost (oxygen depletion or shark collisions).
