## What it “does”, concretely (purely from the code)
- It is a **reactive classifier**: on every step it reads a set of pairwise relations/distances (`LT(...)`, `D(...)`, `ED(...)`, `C(...)`, plus a few raw coords like `Obj3.x`, `Obj12.x`, `Obj11.x#1`) and returns `UP`, `DOWN`, or `NOOP`.
- **Most leaves return `UP`**, many return `DOWN`, and **`NOOP` happens only in specific guarded situations**, e.g.:
  - `ED(Obj10, Obj11) <= 28` and `C(Obj9, Obj1).y <= 118` (in the `LT(Obj1,Obj3).x<=0`, `LT(Obj1,Obj4).x<=0`, `LT(Obj10,Obj1).y<=-3855`, `D(Obj3,Obj6).x<=55`, `ED(Obj3,Obj1)>79` subtree) ⇒ `NOOP`.
