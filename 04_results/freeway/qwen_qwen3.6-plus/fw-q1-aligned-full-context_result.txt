This policy functions as a reactive, multi-lane gap-finding controller. It continuously evaluates precise spatial and distance metrics between the chicken and multiple nearby vehicles across different lanes. When these metrics indicate a sufficiently large safe opening ahead (i.e., nearby cars are far enough away or positioned to allow safe passage), the policy commands `UP` to advance. When vehicles are too close or an imminent collision is detected, it commands `DOWN` to retreat to a safer lane or `NOOP` to wait for traffic to clear. By checking complex multi-vehicle configurations against fixed distance thresholds, the policy times its movements to weave through traffic clusters efficiently, prioritizing collision avoidance while maximizing forward progress to complete crossings and earn points.
