This policy functions as a reactive, threshold-based collision avoidance controller. It continuously evaluates relative position and distance metrics between the controlled chicken (`Chicken1`) and nearby vehicles (`Car1`–`Car10`). When these checks indicate a clear path (vehicles are sufficiently distant or laterally offset), the policy outputs `UP` to advance across lanes. If a vehicle is too close or directly aligned with the chicken's trajectory, it triggers `DOWN` to retreat or `NOOP` to wait for a safe gap. By also monitoring inter-car spacing and the position of the second chicken (`Chicken2`), the policy dynamically times its movements to exploit traffic openings, prioritizing safe, incremental lane crossings over speed.
