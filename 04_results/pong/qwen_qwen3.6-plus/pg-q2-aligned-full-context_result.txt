This policy functions as a highly granular, predictive tracking controller for the player’s paddle. It continuously evaluates the relative vertical positions, distances, and velocities between the ball, the player’s paddle, and the opponent’s paddle. By branching on precise spatial offsets, kinematic data, and historical states, the agent anticipates the ball’s trajectory and commands the paddle to move (LEFT/RIGHT) to intercept it. When the paddle is already aligned with the ball’s path, it outputs NOOP to hold position. The policy also incorporates FIRE actions, likely for serving or triggering game-specific states. Overall, it implements a reactive yet anticipatory interception strategy that dynamically adjusts paddle movement based on exact ball-paddle alignment, opponent positioning, and ball velocity to maximize successful returns and score points.
